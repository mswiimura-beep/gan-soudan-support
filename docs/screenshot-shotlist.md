# Screenshot Shot List

Use this after the app can run on a healthy Simulator or real device.

Draft captures from the current Simulator pass are stored in:

`docs/app-store-screenshots/`

These are review drafts, not guaranteed App Store Connect final-resolution assets.

## Screenshot 1: Home

Purpose: Show product positioning and first actions.

Prepare:

- Consent completed
- At least one upcoming consultation note
- At least two recent notes

Capture:

- `がん相談サポート`
- `このアプリの位置づけ`
- `今日できること`
- `次の診察・相談予定`

Caption draft:

不安や疑問を、診察前に整理。

## Screenshot 2: Write Consultation Note

Purpose: Show low-friction note creation.

Prepare:

- Open `相談を書く`
- Show `よくある相談`
- Select or display templates

Capture:

- Templates for side effects, money/work, family, second opinion
- Category and role fields if visible

Caption draft:

短いメモから、聞きたいことを整理。

## Screenshot 3: Consultation Organization

Purpose: Show supportive conversation style.

Prepare:

- Use a non-urgent sample note about side effects or explanation gap
- Open note detail or question list

Capture:

- `受け止め`
- `気持ち`
- `言い換え`
- `次に聞きたいこと`

Caption draft:

診断せず、相談内容を言葉に。

## Screenshot 4: Question List

Purpose: Show clinician and consultation center questions.

Prepare:

- Save a note about cost/work or side effects
- Open `質問リスト`

Capture:

- `主治医に聞くこと`
- `がん相談支援センターに相談できること`
- PDF/text/share/copy controls if visible

Caption draft:

主治医や相談員に聞くことを準備。

## Screenshot 5: Privacy And Safety

Purpose: Show safety boundary and privacy posture.

Prepare:

- Open Settings
- Navigate to `アプリの位置づけ`, `プライバシー要点`, or `緊急時の案内`

Capture:

- `診断名、再発、病状、緊急性を判定しません`
- `外部サーバー、AIサービス、広告、分析基盤へ送信しません`
- `このアプリは緊急性の判定を行いません`

Caption draft:

診断・治療判断をせず、相談準備に集中。

## Avoid In Screenshots

- Urgent or self-harm example text in App Store screenshots
- Real patient names, hospital names, dates, diagnosis details
- Any statement implying diagnosis, treatment recommendation, medication adjustment, or emergency triage
