# Source And Review Register

This register tracks what the app relies on and what must be reviewed before public release.

## Current App Content Type

- Rule-based consultation organization
- Question templates for clinicians and cancer consultation support centers
- Links to public or specialist information sources
- Privacy and safety boundary text

The app does not currently contain original medical articles.

## Review Required Before Release

- Cancer consultation support wording
- Emergency and self-harm routing wording
- Privacy policy and consent wording
- App Store medical app positioning
- Link validity
- PDF/text export content
- Data deletion flow

## Do Not Add Without Review

- Original treatment explanations
- Drug information
- Prognosis, recurrence, survival probability wording
- Hospital rankings or performance comparisons
- Free medical treatment, clinic ads, or testimonial-based claims
- AI-generated medical answers without source display and output guardrails
